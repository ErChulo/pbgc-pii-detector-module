PII handling workflow

  1. Detect in contextExtract text locally and locate direct, linkable, financial, tax, credential, and technical identifiers.
  2. Report findingsRecord file, type, match preview, context, confidence, and policy basis.
  3. Value severityAssign low, medium, high, or critical severity using type and context.
  4. Manage decisionsReview every finding and choose retain, redact, erase, false positive, or unresolved.
  5. Dispose or redactExport a redacted package, report, and manifest for downstream modules. Sensitive packages require password protection.

Policy basis: PBGC IM 05-09 Privacy Program and IM 10-03 Protecting Personally Identifiable Information. Use synthetic data for testing. Removal or external dissemination of PII requires PBGC authorization and approved transfer protection.
